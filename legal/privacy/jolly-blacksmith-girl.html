Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is handled in connection with the mobile game Adventures of the Jolly Blacksmith Girl (short: Jolly Blacksmith Girl) (the “Game”), developed and published by jellymiso, jellydevelops, and jellydevelops.com (interchangeably referred to as the "Developer"). This Policy applies only to the Game on Android and does not govern third‑party services or advertisements shown inside the Game.

Scope

This Policy describes: (1) the Game’s own data practices; and (2) third‑party data practices that may occur as a result of advertisements shown inside the Game. It does not apply to data collection by ad networks or other third parties, which are governed by those parties’ privacy policies.

Intended audience

The Game is intended for players aged 3 and older. It is not directed specifically to children under the applicable minimum age; parents and guardians should determine suitability for younger players.

Quick summary

  • The Game itself does not collect or store personal data.
  • The Game displays third‑party advertisements; those ad providers may collect data independently.
  • The Developer does not provide a direct contact channel for privacy requests.

Data collected by the Game

None. The Game does not request permissions, nor does it collect, access, or store:

  • Contacts, address book, or call history
  • Location (precise or approximate)
  • Camera, microphone, or photos/media
  • Account credentials or profile information
  • Device identifiers, telemetry, analytics, or crash logs
  • Payments or billing information

Third‑party advertising

The Game includes third‑party advertisements served by independent ad networks. Those ad networks operate independently from the Developer and may collect and process information from your device for purposes including ad delivery, personalization, measurement, and fraud prevention. Typical data that ad networks may collect includes advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID), IP address, device information, inferred interests, and interaction signals.

The Developer does not control the data practices of ad networks. For information about what data an ad provider collects and how to opt out, consult the ad overlay (for example, the “Why this ad?” control), the ad provider’s published privacy policy, and your device/platform privacy controls.

Data sharing and disclosures

By the Game: None — the Game does not collect data to share.

By third parties: Ad networks and their partners may share data with advertisers, measurement vendors, analytics providers, and law enforcement when required by law. Any such sharing is governed by those third parties’ policies.

Retention

The Developer does not retain personal data because the Game does not collect personal data. Any data collected by ad networks is retained according to those providers’ retention policies; consult the ad network’s documentation for details.

User rights and choices

Because the Developer does not collect personal data via the Game, the Developer cannot provide copies, corrections, or deletions of developer‑held data. To exercise rights over data collected by ad networks or other third parties, use the privacy controls offered by those providers and platform settings.

  • Android Advertising ID: Reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings.
  • Ad overlay controls: Use “Why this ad?” or similar in the ad UI to access provider opt‑out options.
  • Platform privacy: Adjust Google/Android privacy and ad personalization settings on your device.

Children

The Game is not targeted to children below the applicable minimum age. The Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe an ad shown in the Game collected data from a child, contact the ad provider directly using the contact or opt‑out links available in the ad overlay.

Security

The Game contains no developer‑held personal data. The Developer follows standard secure development practices to reduce risks in the Game binary and in ad integration. Users should maintain device security and platform updates to reduce risk.

International transfers

The Developer does not transfer personal data because the Game does not collect personal data. Ad networks and other third parties may transfer data across borders; consult those providers’ privacy policies for information about transfer mechanisms and safeguards.

Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be updated periodically. The current version and effective date will be posted at https://jellydevelops.com. Continued use of the Game after a change indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.

If Game functionality changes (for example, new permissions, analytics, sign‑in, payments, or data collection are added), the Developer will update this Policy and the app store listing prior to releasing the changed Game.

Contact

The Developer does not provide a direct contact channel for privacy requests. For concerns about data collected by advertisements shown in the Game, use the ad overlay controls or the ad provider’s published privacy resources, and use your device/platform privacy settings (Android) to limit ad personalization.

Suggested store listing statements

Short store listing: “Jolly Blacksmith Girl does not collect personal data; third‑party ads shown in the game may collect data under their own policies.”

Play Store data safety suggestion: “No developer‑collected data. Third‑party ad network data collection may occur; see ad provider policies.”
