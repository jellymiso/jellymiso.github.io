Neatly organise web pages with Sitefinity

Building & Construction Authority (BCA) is a statutory board in Singapore for the governance of buildings and constructions. The BCA website is built with a customized Sitefinity CMS and consist of the BuildSG microsite, as well as multiple web pages that are neatly organised to provide convenient access to the detailed information available.

Key Takeaway

BCA is the first Sitefinity CMS system that I worked on whereby my tasks involve the building of templates and some system integration process. One of the development highlights would be the customised news listing on the homepage where LINQ was used in the end to retrieve data alongside Sitefinity APIs. Overall, it was challening to build customised functionalities on Sitefinity, perhaps due to the lack of or otherwise poorly explained documentation on the official documentation website.
