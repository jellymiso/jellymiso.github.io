Simple animations bring a better experience for the audience

Nalati is a uniquely themed restaurant and subsidiary of the Distrii Singapore brand. It combines the garden-themed experience with casual dining, experiential online shopping, and physical 4-D digital art in space. Corresponding to the physical outlet, simple fade animations were applied on the homepage to bring a better online viewing experience

Key Takeaway

Although the Nalati website is made with just simple HTML pages, it was the last and one of the best interactive website I have developed as part of the WhooshPro team. One of the key development highlights with this project would be the duet menu carousel on the homepage due to the initial need to cater for responsive and technical requirements from the client. Overall, I enjoyed working on this project especially because the colour scheme comprised of my favourite colours.
